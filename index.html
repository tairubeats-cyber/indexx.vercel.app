About INDEXX

The Mission

INDEXX is a nostalgia archive — a living catalog of the shows, sounds, and signals from the Jetix era that shaped a generation. Part museum, part mixtape, part time machine. We don't just remember the culture — we index it, preserve it, and keep the signal alive.

Why It Exists

Because Saturday mornings meant something. Because Alpha Teens on Machines, Power Rangers, and every show in between weren't just entertainment — they were a shared language. INDEXX exists so none of that gets lost in the static.

What We're Building

A platform that bridges the gap between the era we grew up in and the culture we're creating now. Shows, music, community — all archived under one roof. The vault keeps growing. The signal stays live.
